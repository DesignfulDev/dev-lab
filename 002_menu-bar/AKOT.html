The Decision

Deciding to intervene on your pre-teen, teen or young adult behaviors or emotional needs and find a residential setting or intervention is a journey.  Use this website to compare options side-by-side, consult a professional and visit any treatment program to assess clinicians, treatment approach, community, academics and overall structure that is practiced 24/7.

We have something for your family system

Within Family Choice Behavioral Healthcare interventions, there is a large continuum of care for troubled teens to young adults.  Most teen treatment programs have accredited high school academics with dynamic clinically based and/or milieu-driven treatment. And most treatment centers include family, individual and group treatment.  Find options for your young adult who may need stabilization, assessment or residential treatment or help them step into community-based treatment, with residential or telephonic support.  Learn the facts about each program's options and compare your treatment choices.
